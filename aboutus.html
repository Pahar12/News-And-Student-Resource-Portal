About Us

Welcome to our News Portal, your one-stop destination for the latest and most relevant updates for B.Tech students. Our mission is to bring you curated news, insightful articles, and resources to support your academic journey and keep you informed about industry trends.

Our Purpose

This website was created by a team of passionate students who understand the need for a focused platform for first-year B.Tech students. From the latest in technology to updates on events and industry news, our goal is to provide reliable and accessible information tailored to your interests and educational needs.

What We Offer

  • Up-to-date News: Stay informed with news from the tech world, finance, politics, and more.
  • Industry Resources: Access to articles, tools, and resources that can enhance your learning and skills.
  • Events & Activities: Know about the latest workshops, seminars, and webinars relevant to B.Tech students.
